\paragraph{CTI object builder.}
The labeling core emits evidence findings, fired relations, a session-level epistemic status, off-ladder metadata, and the resulting signal stream. A thin CTI-object builder then transforms this bounded evidence result into a STIX-mappable intermediate object. The builder does not change the evidence decision and does not infer intent. It adds three operational fields required for sharing: confidence, actionability, and shareability. Confidence is calculated from epistemic status, telemetry-profile reliability, evidence specificity, interaction depth, and relation support, with a hard cap derived from the epistemic status. Shareability is calculated as a disclosure-risk score over the evidence roles and fired relations; the object explicitly separates shareable behavioral evidence from non-shareable deployment context such as exact decoy names, credential values, sensor identity, decoy graph design, and matched real-twin information.
